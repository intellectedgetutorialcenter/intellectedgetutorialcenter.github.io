CODE OF CONDUCT & ETHICS

This platform's primary goal is to provide quality service at the right price where employees and clients can develop a professional and trustworthy connection in the wondrous pursuit of learning.

To achieve this, we will always consider the following Code of Ethics while conducting business:

  1. Commitment to Education
    • Dedicated to the pursuit of knowledge and the academic growth of our students.
    • Committed to providing quality education and resources to support students in their learning journey.
  2. Punctuality
    • Aim to be punctual for classes, tutoring sessions and appointments.
  3. Confidentiality
    • Maintain the confidentiality of student records and personal information, and we do not disclose such information without proper authorization.
  4. Professionalism
    • Conduct ourselves in a professional manner at all times and uphold the reputation of the tutorial center.
    • Are committed to continuous professional development to stay current in our fields.
  5. Fair Treatment
    • Treat all students and staff fairly and equitably, ensuring equal opportunities and a safe, supportive learning environment.
  6. Honest & Integrity
    • Uphold the highest standards of honesty, integrity, and ethical conduct in all our interactions and communications.
    • Do not engage in or tolerate any form of cheating, plagiarism, or academic dishonesty.
  7. Safe and Supportive Environment
    • Maintain a safe, inclusive, and supportive learning environment.
    • Avoid any behavior that may create a hostile, offensive, or unsafe atmosphere.
  8. Accountability
    • Hold ourselves accountable for our actions and decisions.
    • Acknowledge and learn from our mistakes to improve our services.
  9. Conflict Resolution
    • Resolve conflicts and disagreements through open communication, mediation, and respectful dialogue.
  10. Compliance to Laws & Regulations
    • Adhere to all relevant laws and regulations governing our educational activities.
    • Committed to ethical business practices.

Following these sets of guidelines will lead to an exemplary platform that provides quality service and will serve as a foundation of newfound learning for all to experience.
